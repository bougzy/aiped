Our Services

The Best Service For You

At AIPED Integrated Services Limited, we offer a wide range of professional services tailored to meet both domestic and industrial needs. Our expertise includes the installation, maintenance, and repair of air conditioners to ensure optimal cooling and efficiency. We also handle the maintenance and repairs of refrigerators, water dispensers, and other household appliances, restoring them to full functionality. In the area of computing, we supply high-quality laptops and desktops and provide dependable maintenance and repair services to keep your systems running smoothly. Our team specializes in safe and reliable electrical installations for both homes and industrial facilities, ensuring compliance with current standards. Additionally, we supply and repair various electronic devices, including modern OLED TVs, offering fast and effective solutions for all brands. We also provide sustainable energy solutions through the supply and maintenance of solar and inverter systems for uninterrupted power. As part of our commitment to community development, we run a skill acquisition programme that empowers individuals with practical training in various technical fields, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed for employment or entrepreneurship.
